# The Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and the objects bound to it by gravity, including **eight planets**, their moons, and other celestial bodies like asteroids and comets.

## The Planets
1. Mercury
2. Venus
3. Earth
4. Mars
5. Jupiter
6. Saturn
7. Uranus
8. Neptune

### Fun Fact
Did you know that **Jupiter** is so massive that it could fit all the other planets inside it?
